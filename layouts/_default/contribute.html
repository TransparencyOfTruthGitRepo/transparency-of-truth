Contribute a Source

Submit a primary source document, court filing, official record, or government publication for review. All submissions are manually verified before publication. Anonymous submissions are accepted.

What we accept

  • Official government documents (PDFs, filings, transcripts)
  • Court records and legal filings
  • Campaign finance records (FEC, state equivalents)
  • Legislative voting records with source links
  • Publicly archived government pages
  • Official meeting minutes and agendas

What we do not accept

  • Opinion pieces, editorials, or commentary
  • Social media posts as standalone evidence
  • Anonymous allegations without documentary support
  • Paywalled content without a public archive link
